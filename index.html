My Very First Post

August 27, 2015 by James

WOO HOO! Welcome to my very first blog post!

Yes, I have been saying for years that I will start a blog. Well now that I am sharpening my skillset in web development I really have no excuse not to. To start off with, I fully expect this site to change, and grow with what I am learning and doing. As of right now I am using Bootstrap as a tool to create this blog, and in particular I am using their example blog as a template. Right this very second this blog is going to look a whole lot like the example. This will be a temporary state as I continue to add and develop ideas for what I want this site to be.

Where it's at

My life is in an exciting period of flux at the moment. I have finally decided to move away from the damned service industry, and actually put this big, grey, mushy mass in my skull to some form of productive use. I started attending courses at Code Fellows here in Seattle. So far I have learned the basics of web development with my interest and point of study being on javascript. I have also learned HTML & CSS within the same courses.

But Why?

I have been needing some movement in my life for a while, and it finally is time. I am getting married soon, and that cetainly is one of my reasons for the change. I also feel that I need to have something to show for my work. I love producing things; from being a baker to being a draftsperson, I have always loved having something to show for my work, and I've missed it. Learning logic through javascript/ jQuery has opened a door to whole new world of creative possibilities. Even more fundamental to me than all that is that I want to do something that I can hang my hat on, to have a level of respect for myself through my work that service can't provide (at least, not for me).
